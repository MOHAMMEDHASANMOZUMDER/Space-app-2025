Subsystem Specifications

Detailed breakdown of modules and features

Intake & Shredder

Primary waste collection and mechanical breakdown system. Processes all incoming waste materials into manageable sizes for further processing.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • Variable feed rate
  • Multi-material compatibility
  • Automated sorting pre-filter

Intelligent Sorting

Advanced AI-powered material classification system using spectral analysis and density separation for optimal processing efficiency.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • 99.7% accuracy
  • Real-time classification
  • Contamination detection

Pyrolysis Chamber

High-temperature thermal decomposition in oxygen-free environment. Breaks down organic compounds into useful gases and carbon residue.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • 450-850¬∞C operation
  • Inert atmosphere
  • Energy recovery system

Catalytic Reforming

Converts pyrolysis gases into hydrogen and carbon monoxide through steam reforming and water-gas shift reactions.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • Ni-based catalysts
  • Steam injection
  • Temperature control

Gas Cleanup

Removes impurities and particulates from reformed gases using multi-stage filtration and scrubbing systems.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • Multi-stage filtration
  • Chemical scrubbing
  • Purity optimization

Condenser System

Separates and recovers water vapor and condensable compounds from the gas stream for reuse in the system.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • Heat recovery
  • Water reclamation
  • Vapor separation

SOFC Power Generation

Solid Oxide Fuel Cells convert clean hydrogen and CO into electricity with high efficiency and minimal emissions.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • 85% efficiency
  • Modular design
  • Long-term stability

Power Management

DC bus system with inverters and battery storage for stable power distribution and energy buffering.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS
  • Smart grid integration
  • Load balancing
  • Energy storage
